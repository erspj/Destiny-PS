Welcome to Destiny Primary School

We are a community-based Primary School located in Wakiso-Mende along Kampala-Hoima, committed to providing quality education to children from all backgrounds.

About Us

Founded in 2009, Destiny Primary School has grown into a vibrant learning center for pupils. We focus on literacy, numeracy, and holistic child development.

Our Programs

  • Early Childhood Education (Nursery)
  • Primary Classes (P1 to P7)
  • Sports and Physical Education
  • Community Service Projects
  • ICT and Digital Learning
